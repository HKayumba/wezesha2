Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does Wezesha differ from other vocational colleges?

Wezesha distinguishes itself through a holistic approach that combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, a commitment to entrepreneurship, and a focus on creating an ecosystem that fosters corporate development and skills acquisition.

2. What programs does Wezesha offer?

Wezesha offers a range of vocational courses, short-term programs, and adopts a unique dual Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) system. Programs cover a spectrum from Financial Intelligence to Corporate Leadership, Teamwork, MS Excel, and Career Counseling.

3. How does Wezesha ensure industry relevance in its curriculum?

Wezesha actively collaborates with local industries, businesses, and employers to establish strong partnerships. The college regularly reviews and updates its curriculum to align with changing industry trends, ensuring students are job-ready upon graduation.

4. Does Wezesha engage with the local community?

Yes, Wezesha is committed to community engagement through partnerships, involvement in community projects, and providing vocational training opportunities to individuals in need.
