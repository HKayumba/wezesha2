About Us

Nurturing Talent, Shaping Futures

Wezesha Vocational College is a transformative institution reshaping technical education in Africa. Inspired by successful vocational systems in Switzerland and Germany, the college integrates entrepreneurship and ethics into its curriculum, emphasizing practical, hands-on training through a dual Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) system. Founded with a vision to be a leader in African technical education, Wezesha actively collaborates with industries, commits to continuous improvement, and fosters an ecosystem that promotes entrepreneurship and skills acquisition. The college's state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and dedication to community engagement distinguish it as a dynamic force empowering students, entrepreneurs, and the broader community with the knowledge and skills needed for success in the evolving job market.

Vision

To be a leader in African technical education by focusing on integrated learning that is ethical, entrepreneurial, innovative and industrious.

Mission

To provide high-quality and ethical technical education to unlock technological advancement careers and industrious African entrepreneurs

Purpose

To work jointly with the Government, corporates, and SMEs to develop relevant technical skills for the economic growth of Namibia.
